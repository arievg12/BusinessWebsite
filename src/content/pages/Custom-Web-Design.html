Engineered Performance - No Page Builders, No WordPress.

At Fern Digital, we build differently. While many freelancers and agencies rely on clunky page builders or aging WordPress templates, we hand-code your site from the ground up. By stripping away the "digital bloat," we deliver websites that perform well.

Our custom-coded approach means:

  1. Instant Load Times: The longer your visitors wait, the greater chance of exiting your site.
  2. Perfect Scores: We aim for 100 on Google PageSpeed Insights, which makes your site rank higher
  3. Security: Without a database or messy plugins, there is quite literally nothing for a hacker to exploit.
  4. Maintenance Fatigue: No more "plugin update" anxiety or broken version conflicts.
